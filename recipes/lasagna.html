Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a traditional Italian dish that is beloved for its rich and comforting flavors. It consists of multiple layers of flat pasta sheets, typically made from wheat flour and eggs, intermingled with various fillings and sauces.

The Lasagna is a traditional Italian dish that is beloved for its rich and comforting flavors. It consists of multiple layers of flat pasta sheets, typically made from wheat flour and eggs, intermingled with various fillings and sauces.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles/li>
  • 1 Pound Ground Meat
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Italian Herbs
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Paramasan Cheese
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Mozeralla Cheese
  • Eggs
  • Fresh Parsley
  • Tomato Paste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C
  2. Cook lasagna noodles until al dente, then drain and set aside
  3. Sauté onions and garlic, then add ground meat and cook until browned. Drain any excess fat. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, herbs, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 15-20 minute
  4. In a separate bowl, combine ricotta cheese, Parmesan cheese, beaten eggs, salt, and pepper.
  5. Layer the lasagna: Start with a thin layer of meat sauce, then add noodles, ricotta mixture, and shredded mozzarella. Repeat until all ingredients are used, ending with a layer of meat sauce and a sprinkle of mozzarella and Parmesan cheese on top.
  6. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes, then remove the foil and bake for an additional 15-20 minutes until the cheese is melted and golden.
  7. Let it rest for 10 minutes, then garnish with parsley if desired. Serve hot and enjoy!
